What to know about µnzn:

µnzn [Münzn] is Austrian for coins, perfectly capturing the idea of our service. It's just like counting coins for the digitalized world. We keep track of your cryptocurrencies so you don't have to. µnzn offers a fast and simple overview of all your stakes in the cryptocurrency world.

"We" are three students of the University of Applied Sciences in Hagenberg, Upper Austria. This WebApp was created as part of a students project during the third semester of the programme Media Technology and Design.
